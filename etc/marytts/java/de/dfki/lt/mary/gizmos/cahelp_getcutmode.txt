GetCutMode or gcm
SetCutMode [mode] or scm [mode]

GetAboveValue or gav
SetAboveValue [value] or sav [value]
GetBelowValue or gbv
SetBelowValue [value] or sbv [value]

For details as to what these values mean and do, see "about".
Mode is a string, either "above", "below" or "both".
The values are float values.

Note: Float value means 
      something like "0.00023" (don't use the german "," version!)
Note: everything (this includes f.e. the parameter [mode]) is
      case insensitive!
